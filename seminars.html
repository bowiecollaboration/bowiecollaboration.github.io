BOWIE+ Seminar Series

 

The BOWIE+ Seminar Series is a monthly seminar highlighting exoplanetary science in the UK and around the world. Each seminar is held on the fourth Thursday of the month at 4pm UK time on Zoom.

If you would like to be informed about our upcoming talks and to receive the Zoom link, please sign up to our mailing list.

We have also set up a calendar so that your diary can be automaticaly updated with upcoming talks (Google Calendar | ICS).
