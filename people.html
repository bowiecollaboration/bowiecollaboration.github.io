BOWIE collaboration members

 

BOWIE began as a JWST GO proposal-writing collaboration between the Universities of Bristol, Oxford, Warwick, Imperial, and Exeter. It has since expanded (+) to include members from many more institutions, including The Open University, University of Leeds, University of Leicester, University of Birmingham, the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy, the Space Telescope Science Institute, and more.
